About Us

The BMA Foundation is a student-led initiative founded by future doctors from TSMU's American MD Program. We are dedicated to making a tangible difference in our communities through transparent giving, impactful volunteering, and creative fundraising events. From supporting medical cases to organizing charity drives, our mission is to empower individuals and foster meaningful connections.

Projects & Events

Winter Warmth Drive

Provided coats, food, and essentials to over 100 families during the cold season.

Student Art Auction

Medical students created and sold original art to raise funds for children’s treatments.

Blood Donation Campaign

Partnered with hospitals to boost donations and spread awareness on donor shortages.
