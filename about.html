Our Story

Founded in 2024 by aspiring doctors at TSMU, the BMA Foundation began as a small group of friends eager to translate medical knowledge into real-world impact.

We operate on three core values: Transparency, Creativity, and Community. Every lari we raise is publicly accounted for. Every event we host blends art, science, and compassion. And every person we help becomes part of our growing family.
